BLAST

BLAST (Basic Local Alignment Search Tool) is a bioinformatics tool developed by
NCBI for comparing biological sequences. Widely used in genomics, it identifies
similarities between a query sequence and a vast database, aiding in the
exploration of functional and evolutionary relationships. With its user-friendly
interface, BLAST is a crucial tool for tasks like gene annotation and sequence analysis.

The pBLAST analysis tool demonstrates that there is only one conserved domain named
.senescence regulator. This protein regulates the expression of proteins associated with
leaf senescence in plants. We performed a protein BLAST as our input consisted of
protein amino acid sequences rather than nucleotide sequences.
